§ 01 — What is OM World?

A self-growing
intent realization network.

OM World is a self-growing intent realization network.

It connects three sides: people who declare intentions; tools, agents, and human services that can deliver them; and a Pattern Library that remembers every successful realization. Each time an intent is realized, the path is saved as a reusable pattern — so the next intent of the same kind is faster, cheaper, and requires less manual thinking. The system grows more capable on its own, and the people who created the underlying patterns are credited each time their work is reused.

The single test every part of the system must pass: does it make future intent realization easier?

OM World 是一个自增长的意图实现网络。它连接意图需求与实现能力，把每一次成功的实现沉淀为可复用模式，让未来同类意图越来越容易实现。成熟形态下，它也是一个去中心化意图经济协议——但 "自增长" 才是核心机制。

01 · DECLARE
Declare.

Users submit real intentions through Submit an Intent. The system classifies what kind of intent it is.

02 · MATCH & EXECUTE
Match & Execute.

A matching engine proposes realization paths from registered tools, agents, and human services. The chosen path runs and records its trace.

03 · REMEMBER
Remember.

Each realization becomes a reusable Pattern. The second intent of the same kind is faster, cheaper, and easier than the first.

§ 01.5 — MVP is live

Open the network
at app.omworld.one.

The Genesis MVP shipped on 2026-05-18. It implements five modules — the minimum needed to test the self-growth thesis:

  1. Intent Demand Entry — Submit an Intent (form + LLM classification)
  2. Capability Supply Entry — Contribute a Capability (Tool / Agent / Human Service)
  3. Matching Engine — LLM-routed, half-automatic path proposals
  4. Pattern Library v0 — every realization becomes a reusable pattern
  5. OM Credit Ledger v0 — internal accounting unit (no token, no on-chain representation)

The first seeded capability is the Genesis Builder Recruitment Generator. Try it: submit "I want to recruit 5 Genesis co-builders for my project" and you'll receive a complete campaign package (positioning, X article, X thread, DM templates, GitHub issue plan, target builder profiles, follow-up plan). The second realization of the same intent type is measurably faster than the first — that's the self-growth loop, validated.

§ 03 — Three principles

Three principles.
One protocol.

Three commitments to users, agents, and the world they act in. None novel alone — together, a contract.

Principle I · 01 / 03

AI is the engine.

AI provides reasoning, planning, generation, coordination, and execution capacity. It is power, not purpose.

Principle II · 02 / 03

Crypto is the law.

Signatures, hashes, attestations, smart contracts, and settlement rules make execution verifiable.

Principle III · 03 / 03

Humanity owns its intentions.

The originator of an intent remains its sovereign owner. Agents act under mandate, not ownership.

§ 04 — Intent Lifecycle

From intention
to verifiable outcome.

Declare. Bind. Route. Execute. Prove. Settle. Remember. Seven stations — each consuming and producing structured evidence.

01 · DECLARE

Declare.

A user expresses an intention.

02 · BIND

Bind.

The intention becomes a signed mandate.

03 · ROUTE

Route.

Agents select tools, compute, and execution paths.

04 · EXECUTE

Execute.

Tools and agents perform the required actions.

05 · PROVE

Prove.

Results are verified through proofs, logs, hashes, or attestations.

06 · SETTLE

Settle.

Payments, rewards, refunds, and reputation are finalized.

07 · REMEMBER

Remember.

The intent becomes part of the originator's owned history.
