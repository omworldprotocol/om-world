§ 01 — What is OM World?

A protocol for the
intent economy.

OM World is a decentralized intent economy protocol.

It enables human intentions to be expressed, transformed into executable actions by AI agents, verified through cryptographic mechanisms, and settled through open economic rules.

OM World 是一个去中心化意图经济协议。AI 将人的意图转化为可执行行动，Crypto 让每一次授权、决策、支付与结果都可验证、可追溯、可共享。

01 · EXPRESS
Express.

Users declare real intentions in natural language or structured form.

02 · EXECUTE
Execute.

AI agents route intents through tools, compute, and services under explicit mandates.

03 · VERIFY
Verify.

Crypto records authorization, execution, proof, settlement, and reputation.

§ 03 — Three principles

Three principles.
One protocol.

Three commitments to users, agents, and the world they act in. None novel alone — together, a contract.

Principle I · 01 / 03

AI is the engine.

AI provides reasoning, planning, generation, coordination, and execution capacity. It is power, not purpose.

Principle II · 02 / 03

Crypto is the law.

Signatures, hashes, attestations, smart contracts, and settlement rules make execution verifiable.

Principle III · 03 / 03

Humanity owns its intentions.

The originator of an intent remains its sovereign owner. Agents act under mandate, not ownership.

§ 04 — Intent Lifecycle

From intention
to verifiable outcome.

Declare. Bind. Route. Execute. Prove. Settle. Remember. Seven stations — each consuming and producing structured evidence.

01 · DECLARE

Declare.

A user expresses an intention.

02 · BIND

Bind.

The intention becomes a signed mandate.

03 · ROUTE

Route.

Agents select tools, compute, and execution paths.

04 · EXECUTE

Execute.

Tools and agents perform the required actions.

05 · PROVE

Prove.

Results are verified through proofs, logs, hashes, or attestations.

06 · SETTLE

Settle.

Payments, rewards, refunds, and reputation are finalized.

07 · REMEMBER

Remember.

The intent becomes part of the originator's owned history.
