01 — Philosophy & Manifesto

A new world enters
through a single point.

OM World is not a product, not a company, not another Web3 launch. It is a protocol for the intent economy: a substrate where human intentions are expressed, executed, and verified — owned by the people who imagined them.

OM World 不是产品、公司、也不是又一个 Web3 项目。它是意图经济的底层协议：人的真实意图在这里被表达、被执行、被验证，并由表达它的人持有。

A world begins as a single point of intention — a seed in mind, before any action.
世界，始于一念。一个意图先于一切行动。
AI is the engine. It does not own the intent — it carries it across distance, language, and time, into action.
AI 是动力源。它不占有意图，它将意图带过时间和距离，化为可执行的动作。
Crypto is the law. Like gravity to a physical world, cryptography to ours: immutable, indifferent, and the same for every participant.
Crypto 是法则。如同物理世界的引力，密码学是这里的引力 — 不可篡改，公平作用于每一个参与者。
Humanity owns its intentions. Authorship is the new property; consent the new boundary; proof the new memory.
人，拥有自己的意图。作者权是新的财产，授权是新的边界，证明是新的记忆。
The OM Credo · 信条
One mind. One real intention, declared, witnessed.
One world. One executable outcome, verifiable by anyone.
Open. No gate-keeper. No closed runtime.
Verifiable. If it cannot be proven, it did not happen.
Composable. Tools, agents, capital — all interchangeable parts.
Slow on values. Fast on execution.
Voice & Tone — Brand Personality
Cosmic, not vague

Speaks at the scale of a world. Never lapses into mood-board mysticism.

Mystical, not religious

Carries spiritual gravity, but is bound by geometry and proof — never iconography.

Technical, not cold

Protocol-grade clarity. Each line should read like a clean spec, but feel inhabited.

Decentralized, not chaotic

Many actors, one law. A council voice — never a brand voice.

03 — Color System

Deep void.
One signal of consciousness.

The palette is a cosmic dark mode with a single warm signal — Consciousness Gold — and a verification accent — Signal Cyan. Indigo is held in reserve. The system avoids the AI-SaaS purple gradient entirely.

All accents share an OKLCH lightness of ~0.78 and chroma of ~0.11; only hue varies. Light mode is a paper-warm off-white, never pure white.

04 — Typography

A protocol speaks plainly.

05 — Graphic Primitives

Ten shapes.
One grammar.

Every brand surface — posters, slides, paper covers, social cards — composes from this small vocabulary. All forms are built on the same geometric grid as the master mark and use the same line weight. Never invent a new ornament.

M-01
Intent Point
The seed. Used to mark the origin of any composition.
M-02
World Field
Concentric worlds. Section backgrounds, paper covers.
M-03
Proof Ring
Verification halo. Twelve ticks, one per proof epoch.
M-04
Agent Path
Intent flowing through tools. Dashed = uncommitted; solid = proven.
M-05
Node Constellation
Open ecosystem map. No center. Highlighted node = active role.
M-06
Hash Trace
A timeline of proofs. Used in protocol diagrams and timestamps.
M-07
Mandate Seal
Authorization stamp. Replaces signatures on consent surfaces.
M-08
Tool Module
An interchangeable unit of capability. Cards in UI follow this geometry.
M-09
Execution Flow
Intent → agent → proof. The narrative spine of every explainer.
M-10
Governance Orbit
Roles orbiting one shared rule. DAO + council visuals.

All ten primitives share a 1.2pt stroke, a 1u/4u radius grid, and the same three-color palette (ink white, gold, cyan). They may combine but never deform — no perspective, no skew, no drop-shadow.

§ Ecosystem Roles · seven seats at one table
Intent Originator

Any person or organization that declares a real intention and stakes it on chain.

AI Agent

09 — Motion Language

Motion is law,
not decoration.

Movement always follows the lifecycle of intent: a point activates, a wave proves outward, a node responds, a path traces, a world settles. Slow, certain, regular. Easings are cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.7, 0, 1) by default. Nothing strobes, nothing bounces.

M-01 · Ignition
A point activates

0 → 600ms. The intent dot fades in over a slow, weighted curve. Used once per page on entry.

M-02 · Resonance
A proof wave

Rings expand outward from the intent. Each ring is one verifier attestation arriving.

M-03 · Trace
An agent path draws

Dashed line draws from intent point to the resolved outcome over 1.2s. Then solidifies once proven.

M-04 · Settle
The world locks

All motion resolves into the master mark. The page is now a verifiable record, no longer animating.

Cadence
Slow, certain

Durations: 300 / 600 / 1200ms — short for state change, long for narrative. Avoid anything < 200ms.

--ease: cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.7, 0, 1)
--ease-out: cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)

Forbidden
No spectacle

No glow strobes. No "matrix" cascades. No parallax. No micro-bounces on hover. No screen-wipe transitions. Motion is the law executing — not an ad.

10 — Brand Rules · Do & Don't

What this brand
refuses to be.

The brief is explicit about traps to avoid: blue-purple AI gradients, cheap crypto metals, religious iconography, cyberpunk neon, busy network spaghetti. The rules below are the operational form of those refusals.

✓ Do · One luminous point per surface

One point of intention

Gold appears once per composition. It is the single point of intent. A second gold element dilutes the metaphor.

✗ Don't · Generic blue-purple gradient

The AI-SaaS purple haze

The single most overused gesture in AI/Web3 branding. OM World is dark, focused, geometric — never atmospheric purple.

✓ Do · Geometric, drawn fresh

Mark is geometry

The logo is always reconstructed from circle + grid + tick. Never traced from a raster. Never wrapped in chrome or bevel.

✗ Don't · Religious or mystical OM

No sacred glyphs

OM is the brand name and a metaphor for an origin sound — never a religious mark. No Sanskrit Aum, no halos, no temple ornament.

✓ Do · Quiet typography
One Mind, One World.

Voice of a council

Headlines are statements of law. Medium weight, tight tracking, sentence case, period at the end. Never marketing-tone.

✗ Don't · Hype / pump tone
🚀 OM TO THE MOON!! 🌙

No rockets, no shouting

OM World does not "moon," "ape," "send it," or address holders. It addresses the people who declared an intent. Same for visuals.

✓ Do · Sparse, legible network

Three nodes, one law

Network diagrams use the minimum nodes needed to express the relationship. Anything more is decoration.

✗ Don't · Neural-network spaghetti

Not a neural-network stock graphic

Avoid the generic "many lines, many dots" AI illustration. Our networks have semantics — every line is a relationship that can be named.

Final Test
Ten questions every artifact must pass.
  1. Does it feel like a world-grade protocol, not an app?
  2. Does it carry AI · Crypto · Intent · World in equal measure?
  3. Is it simple enough to live for ten years?
  4. Is there gravity without religion?
  5. Is there technical credibility without coldness?
  6. Does it travel across cultures?
  7. Will it be read by a developer, an investor, and a poet alike?
  8. Does it work in favicon · token · X · paper · UI?
  9. Does it look unlike any standard AI or Web3 project?
  10. Does it support the line: One Mind, One World?
