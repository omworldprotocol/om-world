# Genesis Questions

> Open questions to resolve before mainnet genesis. Each should end with either a decision (linked to a spec PR) or an explicit deferral.

## Protocol

1. **Token or no token?** Is a native unit required for bonds and fees, or do stablecoins + reputation suffice?
2. **What is the minimum viable proof?** Can we ship without TEE attestations, relying on optimistic disputes alone?
3. **Plan privacy vs. transparency.** How much of the plan must be public at mandate time?
4. **Multi-agent mandates.** First-class, or compose via sub-mandates?
5. **Off-chain tool attestation.** How do we onboard APIs that cannot sign their responses?

## Governance

6. **Founding stewards.** Who, how many, with what term limits?
7. **Council formation trigger.** Time-based, milestone-based, or both?
8. **Treasury constitution.** What can the treasury fund, and what is permanently out of scope?

## Economics

9. **Default fee split.** Is 60/25/10/5 right? On what basis?
10. **Cold-start subsidies.** Should the treasury subsidize early tool providers? If yes, sunset rule?

## Brand & community

11. **License.** MIT, Apache-2.0, BSL with a conversion date, or something else?
12. **Working language.** English-primary with translated mirrors, or multilingual from day one?
13. **Where do discussions live?** GitHub issues, a forum, a chat — or all three with clear roles?
